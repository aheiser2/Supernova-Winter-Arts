Supernova auditions are fun and casual! You'll learn original dance and flag choreography, meet new people, and grow your skills! By the end of the first day you'll have a handle on the skills needed for week two's audition, which you can spend practicing throughout the week leading up to it; week 2's audition date will consist of learning the routine, practicing for an hour or so, and then small group performances assessed by the Supernova staff. We will be looking for hard working, passionate team players that enjoy a friendly learning environment and those willing to contribute their best effort consistently. Your work ethic and how well you work with others factor heavily in our decision on if you make it.

Audition Experience: Not auditioning, but want to see what it's like or use the audition as a spin clinic? Select "Experience/Practice" when registering.

Please wear all black, form-fitting, and non-revealing attire so that staff are able to view your body lines. Hair should be pulled back, out of the face, and secured. You may dance in indoor dance shoes or bare feet, depending on your preference.

Please bring your own equipment & water, as well as snacks if desired. Bring kneepads and any wraps that are needed for support. Remember, this is a SPORT!
