Help us keep reaching for the stars by giving today!

We cannot do this alone!

Supernova Winter Arts relies on direct donations from fans, alumni, and patrons of the arts to provide the resources necessary to make our program possible - and unfortunately, winter guard is not a cheap activity! Expenses each season include competition entry fees, rehearsal facility fees, equipment, costumes, staff, design, insurance, and more. Your donations help cover the cost of these so that we can keep our member's season fees low. Whether you are able to give a little or a lot, every dollar donated helps us give young people in Lincoln and the surrounding communities the opportunity to create, collaborate, and develop life-long skills by participating in the arts. You make Supernova possible!

When you give to Supernova Winter Arts, you can directly see the impact of your gift in the performance of our winter guard ensemble. What you won't see are the life-changing experiences you are providing to children and young adults behind the scenes, by allowing them to connect as a team, be active, and experience the power of the arts first-hand!


There are many ways you can support Supernova Winter Arts:

  • Make a one-time donation
  • Budget your giving and set up a recurring donation
  • Volunteer your time or effort
  • Support any of our fundraisers featured below!

Remember, every little bit helps us make this program possible!
