Volunteer for Supernova!

Both on the performance floor and off, Supernova Winter Arts is always in need of people who believe in our mission and are willing to volunteer their time and talents to support our cause. Whether it's helping us with uniforms and equipment, assisting at rehearsals or an event, or helping us up our social media game, there's plenty of ways you can help out.

Have a skill and/or professional service that you can offer? We could use your help! We have many organizational needs such as:


  • graphic design
  • video editing
  • sewing/tailoring
  • prop building/designing
  • grant writing
  • tax preparation
  • legal services
  • insurance
  • almost anything you can think of!

Anything you are willing to do to help make our season happen will be greatly appreciated - your professional skills and expertise really can make a difference! Fill out the form below to get connected with Supernova Winter Arts and stay up to date on any upcoming opportunities, or email us at supernovawinterarts@gmail.com to learn more.
