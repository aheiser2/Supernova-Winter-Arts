Contribution Levels

Sabre Level - Platinum

Donors who have contributed $2000 or more (excluding company matching) within a calendar year will receive the following as a special "thank you" for their extraordinary generosity:

  • An ad spot in the footer of the Supernova Winter Arts website under "Our Partners & Supporters"
  • A LARGE spot on the back of the current year's show shirt for their name/company
  • A VIDEO social media spotlight, including their company logo
  • A spot on Supernova's online Donor Wall

Rifle Level - Gold

Donors who have contributed $750 to $1999 (excluding company matching) within a calendar year will receive the following as a special "thank you" for their outstanding generosity:

  • An ad spot in the footer of the Supernova Winter Arts website under "Our Partners & Supporters"
  • A MEDIUM-sized spot on the back of the current year's show shirt for their name/company
  • A static social media spotlight, including their company logo
  • A spot on Supernova's online Donor Wall

Flag Level - Silver

Donors who have contributed $250 - $749 (excluding company matching) within a calendar year will receive the following as a special "thank you" for their greatly appreciated generosity:

  • A SMALL spot on the back of the current year's show shirt for their name/company
  • A static social media spotlight, including their company logo
  • A spot on Supernova's online Donor Wall

Dance Level - Bronze

Donors who have contributed $50 - $249 (excluding company matching) within a calendar year will receive the following as a special "thank you" for their appreciated generosity:

  • A static social media spotlight, including their company logo
  • A spot on Supernova's online Donor Wall
