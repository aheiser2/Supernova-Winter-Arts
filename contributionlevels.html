Corporate Sponsorship Levels

Corporate sponsors help us keep the show going by helping us pay for things like uniforms, flags, practice space, and props! Want to become a corporate sponsor? Click here, or read about our different sponsorship levels and their benefits below.

Sabre Level - Platinum

Sponsors who contribute $2000 or more within a calendar year (Nov. 1st - Oct. 31st) will receive the following as a special "thank you" for their extraordinary generosity:

  • A logo or ad spot in the footer of the Supernova Winter Arts website under "Our Partners & Supporters" - seen by anyone who visits our site
  • A spot on the back of the current year's show shirt for their name/company, in a LARGE font
  • A VIDEO social media spotlight, including their company logo
  • A spot on Supernova's online Donor Wall, including a link to their website - allows people to click directly to get to their company's information

Rifle Level - Gold

Sponsors who contribute $1000 to $1999 within a calendar year (Nov. 1st - Oct. 31st) will receive the following as a special "thank you" for their outstanding generosity:

  • A spot on the back of the current year's show shirt for their name/company, in a MEDIUM font
  • A photo social media spotlight, including their company logo
  • A spot on Supernova's online Donor Wall, including a link to their website - allows people to click directly to get to their company's information

Flag Level - Silver

Sponsors who contribute $500 - $999 within a calendar year (Nov. 1st - Oct. 31st) will receive the following as a special "thank you" for their greatly appreciated generosity:

  • A spot on the back of the current year's show shirt for their name/company, in a SMALL font
  • A photo social media spotlight, including their company logo
  • A spot on Supernova's online Donor Wall

Dance Level - Bronze

Sponsors who contribute $100 - $499 within a calendar year (Nov. 1st - Oct. 31st) will receive the following as a special "thank you" for their appreciated generosity:

  • A photo social media spotlight, including their company logo
  • A spot on Supernova's online Donor Wall
