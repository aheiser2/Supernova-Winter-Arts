Donation Options

Donate to the General Fund

While our members pay participation dues, those fees don't cover all of our operational expenses... donations, fundraisers and sponsors help make up the difference. Your charitable gift will allow Supernova to continue offering youth and young adults in south-east Nebraska and the surrounding area. A general fund donation will help us pay for rehearsal space, equipment, staff pay, props/accessories, and other things as needed. Thank you for donating to Supernova Winter Guard! Your support, no matter the size, truly makes a huge impact on our organization and the communities we serve.

Fund a Member

Supernova travels to compete with other color guards in the mid-west region. As with any competitive team, there are membership costs to participate including uniform expenses, equipment, competition fees, facility rental fees, etc. If you'd like to sponsor a specific member of our guard, choose this option to make your contribution go towards covering your selected member's dues for the season!

Donate products, prizes, and more!

We are always in need of material goods throughout the season. Please email us at supernovawinterarts@gmail.com if you're interested in donating something!

Here are a few of our common needs:

  • Materials for set construction (paint, wood, hardware, tools)
  • Items for team meals (non-perishables, plasticware, catered meals)
  • Materials for costume creation (notions, fabric, sewing machines)
  • Fundraiser door prizes (electronics, home goods, gift cards)
  • New, or gently used, equipment (rifle, sabre, flag)
